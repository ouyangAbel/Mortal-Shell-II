Weapon System Overview

Mortal Shell II features 14 weapons across 6 categories. Every weapon can be upgraded at the Blazing Forge using Embers, up to +10. At +5, each weapon unlocks its second skill (special attack). Each weapon has two skill slots, unlocked by defeating specific enemies or collecting skill scrolls.

Weapon choice fundamentally shapes your playstyle — heavy weapons hit hard but are slow; light weapons are agile but require more hits. Each Shell's stamina pool determines how many attacks you can chain.

Straight Swords

The most balanced weapon type — fast attacks, moderate stamina cost, maximum adaptability. Suits every Shell. The best choice for beginners.

Skills: 1. Mechanical Thrust — quick stab followed by backstep; 2. Soul Echo — small AoE soul explosion on hit.

Verdict: The do-everything sword. Viable from start to finish. The stamina regen at +10 is invaluable in endurance fights. If this is your first playthrough, you can't go wrong with this.

Greatswords

Greatswords deliver massive damage with strong stagger. Tradeoff: slow speed and extreme stamina cost. Best with power-focused Shells (Eredrim).

Location: Dropped by Crucix (Crypt of the Damned boss).

Skills: 1. Earthshatter — slams ground for a forward shockwave; 2. Frost Enchant — temporarily imbues with ice damage.

Verdict: If you love one-shotting enemies, this is your weapon. The charged heavy knockback gives you breathing room against groups. The slow speed demands mastery of Harden timing.

Location: Abyssal Rift hidden area — before The Unchained's boss room, take the branching path to the "Shadow Corridor." Defeat the hidden boss "Abyssal Knight." Requires the "Shadow Key" (found on the top floor of the Watcher's Spire).

Skills: 1. Shadow Rush — teleport behind target and attack; 2. Abyssal Vortex — creates a shadow vortex that pulls enemies in.

Verdict: The ultimate late-game greatsword. Lifesteal + Harrow Shell makes you nearly unkillable. Acquiring it is a serious challenge — you must deviate from the main path and the hidden boss is no joke.

Scythes

Scythes specialize in wide-area attacks and AoE damage. Great for dense enemy packs and large bosses where you can multi-hit.

Max (+10)24018/hitMediumSweep range +40%, bleed effect

Location: Chest in the Watcher's Spire mid-levels. Third floor, small room off the spiral staircase.

Skills: 1. Spinning Reap — 360° spin attack; 2. Soul Harvest — massive heal on enemy kill.

Verdict: The ultimate mob-clearing weapon. Wide sweeps let you control groups effortlessly. Also solid against large bosses with multiple hitboxes.
