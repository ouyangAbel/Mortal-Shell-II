Any% Route — Fastest Path to Credits

The Any% route bypasses all optional content. Only 3 bosses are mandatory: Crucix (Crypt of Martyrs), Imrod (Seat of Infinity), and The Dark Father (Shifting Archives). Tarsus and Corvid can be skipped entirely using the Temple Grounds elevator skip.

Optimal Shell & Weapon: Eredrim with Smoldering Mace. Eredrim's damage stacking kills bosses in the fewest attack cycles. Smoldering Mace's AoE clears mobs between bosses without slowing down for individual kills.

Route summary:

  1. Fallgrim (0:00-3:00): Grab Hallowed Sword. Skip Tiel/Harrow Shells — stick with Harros. Run directly to Temple Grounds gate.
  2. Temple Grounds (3:00-8:00): Sprint through Outer Sanctum → Foundry → Inner Sanctum. Ignore all enemies. Take the Crypt elevator. Activate Genessa at Crypt entrance. Skip: Do not kill Flame Wardens. Run past them.
  3. Crypt of Martyrs (8:00-15:00): Grab Martyr's Blade from hidden wall. Kill Crucix (parry bait, ~2 min fight). Warp back to Fallgrim.
  4. Seat of Infinity (15:00-25:00): Elevator down → Forge District → Lava Arena → Kill Imrod (frost weakness, ~3 min fight). Grab Forge Key. Warp to Temple Grounds.
  5. Temple Grounds Foundry (25:00-28:00): Use Forge Key to access Magma Gate. Grab Smoldering Mace. Warp to Fallgrim.
  6. Shifting Archives (28:00-45:00): Archives gate is now open (3 bosses killed). Sprint through Lower → Upper Level. Skip Abyssal Siphon. Kill The Dark Father (~8 min fight).
  7. Credits (45:00-48:00): Skip cutscenes.
Pro Insight: The Temple Grounds elevator skip saves ~8 minutes by bypassing Tarsus. After activating the Crypt Genessa, do NOT take the right elevator to Seat of Infinity. Instead, warp to Fallgrim and use the Outer Ring to reach the Seat of Infinity directly via the southwest gate — this skips the Inner Sanctum Tarsus trigger entirely.

FAQ

What is the current Any% world record?

Approximately 48 minutes (varies by patch). The record relies on the Tarsus/Corvid double skip, Forge Key warp, and optimized Eredrim Smoldering Mace boss kills.

Which Shell is best for speedrunning?

Eredrim. Annihilator skips the final 10% of The Dark Father (saves ~2 minutes alone). Brutal Strikes + Executioner provide the highest damage-per-swing for minimal upgrade investment. Tiel is competitive in All Bosses (Shadow Step for mobility between bosses) but loses time on Dark Father.

Do I need to upgrade weapons for speedruns?

Minimum: Smoldering Mace Tier 3 (requires 4× Ember Core from Seat of Infinity). The route naturally collects enough Ember Cores from the Forge District clear. Tier 5 is ideal but requires additional farming time that offsets the damage gain — only worth it for WR attempts.

Can I skip the Shifting Archives Gate requirement?

No. The gate requires 3 boss kills to unlock (Crucix + Imrod + Tarsus or Corvid). In Any%, killing Tarsus is slower than killing Corvid — most runners kill Corvid instead because her boss arena is closer to a Genessa shrine, reducing travel time despite the fight being slightly longer.
