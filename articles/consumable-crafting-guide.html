Crafting Basics — Where & How to Craft

Crafting in Mortal Shell II is performed at the Twin-Faced Merchant in Fallgrim's Outer Ring (near the Temple Grounds gate) or at any Sester Genessa shrine after acquiring the Mortar & Pestle (found in the Crypt of Martyrs main chamber). The Merchant sells ingredients; Genessa shrines allow crafting from your inventory. Crafting consumes ingredients but no Tar — it is purely material-based.

Key tip: The Merchant's ingredient stock refreshes after each boss kill. Buy out his stock after every boss — especially Iron Shards (200 Tar) and Healing Herbs (150 Tar). You will need these in bulk for consumable crafting.

FAQ

What is the single most important consumable?

Iron Bark (20% DR for 120s). It is the difference between surviving 3 boss hits and 5. In NG+ cycles where boss damage scales aggressively, Iron Bark is mandatory for every fight. Always carry 3.

How do I farm Oil Flasks efficiently?

Purchase from the Twin-Faced Merchant (300 Tar each). His stock of 3 Oil Flasks refreshes after every boss kill. After clearing the game once, you have ~30 Oil Flasks from merchant purchases alone. There is no enemy that drops Oil Flasks — purchase is the only reliable source.

Can I stack multiple weapon enchantments?

No. Applying Fire Oil overwrites Frost Vial and vice versa. Poison Vial can coexist with Fire or Frost (it applies a separate status). The optimal combination is Fire Oil + Poison Vial for maximum DoT stacking, or Frost Vial + Sharpening Stone for crit builds.

Do consumable buffs persist through death?

No. All active buffs are lost on death. Re-apply before every boss attempt. This is why consumable farming matters — dying 10 times to The Dark Father means consuming 10 sets of buffs.

What is the best consumable for speedruns?

Fire Oil + Stamina Tonic. Fire Oil provides the largest single damage boost (+20%). Stamina Tonic lets you chain more attacks per stamina bar, reducing downtime. Skip Iron Bark in speedruns — you should not be getting hit.
