Boss Rush Overview — Rules & Reward Tiers

Boss Rush mode unlocks after defeating The Dark Father on any difficulty. Access it from the main menu or from the Fallgrim Central Tower (new portal behind Genessa). You fight all 8 bosses consecutively with no healing between fights — HP, Resolve, and consumables carry over. Your Shell and weapon loadout is locked for the entire run (no swapping at shrines — there are no shrines).

Reward tiers (based on completion time):

Pro Insight: Boss HP in Boss Rush is scaled to NG level regardless of your current NG+ cycle. This means NG+7 characters with maxed weapons and Shells will absolutely demolish the early bosses — Hadern dies in 3 charged heavies. The challenge is endurance and consumable management, not raw boss difficulty.

Optimal Shell & Weapon Route for S-Rank

Shell: Eredrim (primary). Harrow (equip before Unchained and Dark Father for Vengeance). Since Boss Rush locks your Shell, you must choose one. Eredrim is mathematically optimal: Annihilator skips the last 10% of Dark Father P3 (saves ~2 minutes), Executioner II accelerates every boss's final 30%, and Brutal Strikes + Rampage provide consistent damage across all 8 fights.

Weapon 1: Smoldering Mace (Tier 5). AoE shockwave for multi-target phases (Tarsus clones, Dark Father clones). Fire damage for Crucix, Tarsus, Hadern. Apply Fire Oil before Crucix — it lasts through Tarsus if you are fast.

Weapon 2: Martyr's Blade (Tier 5). Swap for Imrod (frost weakness +25%). Frostbite slow for Unchained and Dark Father. The 1.8s charged windup is manageable because Eredrim's Colossus prevents stagger.

FAQ

What is the hardest boss in Boss Rush?

Corvid at position 5. You have already burned some consumables on Imrod (fight 4), and her Shroud of Darkness phase adds 60-90 seconds of unavoidable waiting — Dive Bombs must be Harden-countered, and you cannot rush her down during Shroud. She is the primary S-Rank killer.

Can I swap weapons during Boss Rush?

Yes — you can swap between your two equipped weapon slots between bosses, but not during fights. This means you can bring Smoldering Mace + Martyr's Blade and swap as needed.

What happens if I die?

You restart from the beginning (Hadern). There are no checkpoints. This is why Vengeance (Harrow) is so valuable — it is effectively a mid-run checkpoint that prevents a full restart.

Is Boss Rush required for platinum?

Yes — the "Gauntlet Master" achievement requires completing Boss Rush with at least B-Rank (35 minutes). S-Rank is NOT required for platinum, only for the Golden Shell cosmetic and bragging rights.

What NG+ cycle should I attempt Boss Rush?

NG (first cycle) or NG+1. Boss HP is always scaled to NG level regardless of your cycle, so higher cycles only benefit you (better gear, higher Shell levels). NG+1 gives you Annihilator and full consumable stocks. Attempt Boss Rush immediately after your first NG+ clear.
