Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Mortal Shell II and the original?

The sequel keeps the core Harden combat while adding a much larger open world, 10+ new weapons, new Shells, deeper skill trees, and roughly 3x the map size with significantly more bosses.

Which Shell should a beginner pick?

We recommend Harrow (the durable Shell). High HP and defense give you room to make mistakes while learning. Once comfortable, switch to Tiel for high-speed combat. See the full Shell Compendium.

How hard is this game? Good for Souls newcomers?

Mortal Shell II sits around the same difficulty as Dark Souls 3. The Harden mechanic gives you a unique defensive tool that's arguably more intuitive than dodge-rolling. If you can adapt to a deliberate pace, you'll pick it up. Our Beginner's Guide smooths the transition.

Can all weapons be collected in one playthrough?

Most can, but 2-3 hidden weapons require specific conditions (hidden bosses or NPC questlines). Check the Weapons Catalog for acquisition details.

Can I respec? Can Shell skills be reset?

Mid-to-late game, you can reset Shell skill points at the Sanctuary using a "Tear of Echoes." These are limited, so plan before spending. Consult our Builds guide first.
