Mortal Shell II Trophy & Achievement Guide

42 Trophies · Platinum Roadmap · Difficult Achievements · Optimal Order

Overview

Mortal Shell II has 42 trophies/achievements (1 Platinum + 3 Gold + 10 Silver + 28 Bronze). Estimated platinum time: 40-50 hours, requiring at least 2 playthroughs (some achievements are mutually exclusive). This guide lists achievements in the recommended unlock order with detailed strategies for the hardest ones.

Platinum roadmap: NG — focus on clearing the story + full collection (except mutually exclusive achievements). NG+ — mop up remaining branch achievements. NG+ carries over all weapons, Shell skill levels, and Embers, so NG+ is actually easier.

Difficult Trophy Walkthroughs

Shell Collector — Obtain all 6 Shells

CollectionDifficulty: ★★★Best in: NG
  1. Harrow: Fallgrim Center, obtained at game start
  2. Solomon: Sarcophagus in Crypt of the Damned upper level
  3. Tiel: Sarcophagus at Watcher's Spire entrance
  4. Eredrim: Beside the giant furnace in Blazing Forge Smelting Quarter
  5. Genessa: Frozen Sanctum side chapel — speak with NPC 3 times
  6. Azuran: Hidden cave at Abyssal Rift's deepest point

Note: Azuran is the easiest to miss — the hidden cave entrance is extremely well concealed. From before The Unchained's boss room, take the path left to its end. Look for a dark wall you can break with your weapon.

Arsenal Complete — Obtain all 14 weapons

CollectionDifficulty: ★★★★Best in: NG

Three easiest to miss:

  • Abyssal Greatsword: Requires Shadow Key from Watcher's Spire, then defeating the hidden Abyssal Knight in the Abyssal Rift's Shadow Corridor. Most commonly missed.
  • Berserker Twin Axes: Optional Berserker Trial in the Abyssal Rift — many players rush to the final boss after beating The Unchained.
  • Blood Moon Scythe: Requires completing the Crimson Priestess NPC questline — if you miss a dialogue at any stage, the quest breaks.

Cross-reference with the Weapons Catalog to confirm each one.

True Ending

EndingDifficulty: ★★★★★Best in: NG+

Mortal Shell II has multiple endings. The true ending requires:

  1. Speak with every NPC until dialogue repeats (at least 3 times each, revisiting at different story stages)
  2. Complete the Crimson Priestess questline (obtaining the Blood Moon Scythe confirms completion)
  3. Defeat both hidden bosses: Frost Wyrm and Abyssal Knight
  4. Before The Dark Father boss fight, carry 4 specific Shells in your inventory (Harrow + Solomon + any 2 others)
  5. Defeat The Dark Father's third phase without using Harden once. Yes, really.

The last condition is the true test. Attempt this on NG+ with fully upgraded gear. Use Genessa Shell with high passive regen to survive without Harden.

No Shell Required — Beat a boss in spirit form

ChallengeDifficulty: ★★★★★Best in: NG+

You must defeat any boss as a pure spirit — no Shell. In spirit form: extremely low HP (~30), no Harden, no skills, restricted weapon access.

Recommended strategy: Choose Hadern (first boss). On NG+ with a maxed weapon, challenge him directly. His attack patterns are the most predictable — memorize each timing. Use the Obsidian Spear for safe distance poking. Bring every healing item you own. Expect 10-15 attempts.

Warning: Do not pick Tarsus or Corvid. Pick the easiest boss. Hadern is the correct answer.

Untouchable — No-hit any boss

ChallengeDifficulty: ★★★★Best in: NG+

Again, pick Hadern. NG+ with a maxed Hollowed Sword or Abyssal Greatsword, paired with Tiel Shell (shortest Harden cooldown). Hadern's rhythm is ideal for no-hit — every attack has clear telegraphing. Key: don't be greedy, hit once per opening, Harden his counter.

If your Harden timing isn't confident, try Genessa Shell + Obsidian Spear — safe distance poking.

Speedrunner — Beat the game in under 3 hours

ChallengeDifficulty: ★★★Best in: NG+

3 hours sounds tight, but with NG+ maxed gear it's generous. Route: Fallgrim → Crypt → Frozen Sanctum → Blazing Forge → Watcher's Spire → Abyssal Rift → Final Boss. Skip all optional content (no hidden weapons, no Shell collection, no questlines).

Recommended setup: Eredrim Shell + Smoldering Mace +10. High damage one-shots everything on the way. Skip all non-mandatory fights. Bosses only. If you're tight on time, use Harrow Shell to tank through zones — dying doesn't cost time (respawn and keep running, Souls don't matter).

Pacifist? — Beat the game without killing non-boss enemies

ChallengeDifficulty: ★★★★Best in: NG+

Pure parkour run. No non-boss kills. Tiel Shell recommended (max speed, shortest Harden CD). Harden through enemy clusters and keep running. Memorize every shortcut route — this determines whether you make it through. If surrounded, Harden and keep running.

Hardest zone: Abyssal Rift (narrow bridges + dense enemies). Don't sweat deaths — dying and retrying doesn't reset the kill count.

Recommended Platinum Order

New Game (~25 hours)

  1. Progress through the story, defeat all 7 bosses
  2. Explore every zone — collect all 6 Shells and as many weapons as possible
  3. Speak with every NPC until dialogue repeats; complete NPC questlines
  4. Defeat both hidden bosses
  5. Upgrade the Hollowed Sword to +10 (unlocks Master of Arms)
  6. Unlock all Harrow skills (unlocks Fully Awakened)
  7. Complete Crimson Priestess questline for Blood Moon Scythe
  8. Beat the game with the normal ending

New Game+ (~15 hours)

  1. Mop up any missing weapons (unlocks Arsenal Complete)
  2. No-hit Hadern (unlocks Untouchable)
  3. Defeat Hadern in spirit form (unlocks No Shell Required)
  4. 3-hour speedrun (unlocks Speedrunner)
  5. Fulfill true ending conditions and beat the game (unlocks True Ending)
  6. Pacifist run (unlocks Pacifist?)
  7. Upgrade remaining weapons to +5 or higher (unlocks Fully Upgraded)
  8. Platinum pops!
